Green Tea and Lemon
If you need a pick-me-up, brew some green tea.
The low-calorie drink is packed with antioxidants called catechins, which may help you burn more calories and fat.
One study suggested that drinking 4 cups of green tea every day may lead to decreases in weight and blood pressure.
To make it even healthier, add a squeeze of lemon -- it helps your body absorb them.
